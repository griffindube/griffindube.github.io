    Teriyaki Tofu Stir Fry

    This recipe makes 4 servings. Ingredients 28oz Firm Tofu 1 tbsp cornstarch 2 cups Sliced Carrots 1lb Broccoli 1/2 yellow onion For Sauce: 1/2 cup Soy Sauce 1/2 cup water 2 tbsp Brown Sugar 1/2 tbsp cornstarch Steps Press Tofu to remove excess moisture. Mix corn starch and bake until crispy. Stir fry veggies in a pan, then add in tofu and sauce. Enjoy :) Macros for 4 Servings Protein: 31g Carbs: 36g Fat: 12g Total Calories: 372

    Creamy Southwest Chicken Chili

    This recipe makes 6 servings. Ingredients 1 1/3 pounds Chicken breast 1 can Corn 1 can Black Beans 1 Red Bell Pepper 1/2 Yellow Onion 2 cups Chicken Broth 9 tsp (1 pkt) Hidden Valley Ranch Seasoning 8 oz Cottage Cheese, regular, 4% Steps Combine everything except for the cottage cheese into a crockpot, cook on low for 4-6 hours. Once 6 hours has passed, remove and shred the chicken breasts, add them back into the crockpot with the cottage cheese.

    Cottage Cheesy Turkey Kale Mix

    This recipe makes 4 servings. Ingredients 1 pound Ground Turkey 2 cups Cottage Cheese, 4% fat 2 cups kale, raw 1 tbsp taco seasoning Steps Brown the ground turkey, mixing in the taco seasoning while it is cooking with a small amount of oil. Once the ground turkey is cooked, add in all of the cottage cheese and mix. Simmer on medium-low heat, stirring every once and a while to make sure nothing is sticking and everything is incorporated.

    Trees by Joyce Kilmer

    I think that I shall never see A poem lovely as a tree. A tree whose hungry mouth is prest Against the earth’s sweet flowing breast; A tree that looks at God all day, And lifts her leafy arms to pray; A tree that may in Summer wear A nest of robins in her hair; Upon whose bosom snow has lain; Who intimately lives with rain. Poems are made by fools like me, But only God can make a tree.
